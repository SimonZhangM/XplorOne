# WAIC FutureTech OPC Deck

This folder contains XplorOne's WAIC FutureTech OPC independent pioneer challenge material.

## What This Is

The PDF is a 9-page Chinese visual deck prepared for WAIC FutureTech OPC review and communication.

It is closer to a concise product pitch deck than a long technical whitepaper.

File:

- [XplorOne-WAIC-FutureTech-OPC-Project-Plan.pdf](./XplorOne-WAIC-FutureTech-OPC-Project-Plan.pdf)

## Core Message

The deck presents XplorOne as:

> a local-first AI finance workspace for one-person businesses, solopreneurs, freelancers, and independent workers.

Its central argument is that many independent workers are not blocked by income itself, but by financial opacity:

- income comes from multiple platforms
- accounts and funds are scattered
- personal spending and business spending are mixed together
- future cash-flow risk is hard to judge in advance

## What the Deck Covers

The PDF focuses on:

- why one-person businesses need finance infrastructure in the AI era
- how XplorOne moves beyond simple bookkeeping toward operating judgment
- the loop from recording to structuring, querying, analysis, decision-making, and back to bookkeeping
- the current desktop product state, including books, accounts, categories, transactions, budgets, charts, cash flow, assets and liabilities
- the separation between Local Assistant, AI Assistant, and external agent access
- the local-first data foundation, including local ledger storage and controlled access boundaries
- read-only MCP access for external agents such as Codex or WorkBuddy
- the fact that XplorOne has moved from idea to runnable desktop product
- the product and commercialization logic for users who need clearer financial visibility and stronger analysis capabilities
